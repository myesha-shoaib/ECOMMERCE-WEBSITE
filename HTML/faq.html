Frequently asked questions

Answers about payment, delivery, returns and accounts.

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery takes 3–5 working days. Express delivery takes 1–2 working days.

Can I place an order without login?

You may add products to the cart without login, but login is required when placing an order.

How do returns work?

Eligible products can be returned within seven days if unused and in original packaging.

Which payment methods are supported?

The demo includes cash on delivery, bank transfer and card payment selections.

Can I cancel an order?

An order can be cancelled while its status is Processing.
